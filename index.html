Civil Air Patrol Subordinate Graphic Generator

This tool allows Civil Air Patrol members to generate compliant logos in accordance with CAP branding guidelines. Follow the steps below to create a professional, standardized logo for your unit, directorate, Special activity, or project.


Step 1: Enter the subordinate entity, or directorate's name

Input your directorate, program, region, wing, group, squadron, or activity name as you want it to appear. Be sure spelling, capitalization, and abbreviations match official Civil Air Patrol naming conventions.

50 characters remaining

Step 2: Choose Colors and Style

Select between the available color options: an all-blue or all-white version of the logo. These simplified options are designed to maintain consistency and ensure clear visibility across different backgrounds and use cases.

Use the blue version for light backgrounds and the white version for dark backgrounds to maintain strong contrast and readability.

Step 3: Review the Preview

Before downloading, examine the preview for spacing, alignment, readability, and overall balance. Make sure the design still looks clear when viewed at smaller sizes.

Step 4: Download Your Finished File

Once you are satisfied with the design, download the logo in the available file format. Use the format most appropriate for your intended purpose, whether digital display, printing, or further graphic design work.
